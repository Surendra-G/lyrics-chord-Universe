About Us

Lyrics & Chords Universe is a website that offers lyrics of the songs and their guitar chords. It focuses on Nepali songs but also includes English and Hindi songs. The site provides easy-to-understand guitar chords played at specific timings. Users can search for songs or browse through collections. Lyrics & Chords Universe embeds YouTube songs for easy listening and offers downloadable guitar tab images. It caters to beginners, intermediate guitar players, and those who want to sing along. The platform is constantly updated with new content and welcomes user support and feedback to enhance its offerings.
